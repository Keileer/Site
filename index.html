about us

We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint by removing the power adapter and EarPods from the iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 mini package. Instead, iPhone comes with a USB ‑ C to Lightning fast charging cable that can be connected to a USB ‑ C power adapter or to a computer port. We recommend using your existing USB ‑ A to Lightning cables, power adapters, and headphones that are compatible with these iPhone models. However, you can purchase new Apple power adapters and headphones if needed. MagSafe is a new ecosystem of accessories that instantly magnetize for faster wireless charging. They can be combined with each other, creating any combination. AppleCare+ for Mac provides up to three years of expert technical support and additional hardware coverage from Apple, including up to two incidents of accidental damage protection every 12 months. Each incident is subject to a service fee of A$149 for screen damage or external enclosure damage, or A$429 for any other damage. In addition, you’ll get 24/7 priority access to Apple experts via chat or phone through getsupport.apple.com. Coverage begins on your AppleCare+ purchase date.
